About Us

Whacked specialises in building meaningful, authentic personal brands through tailored content creation - designed to nurture an audience to
generate inbound leads for your business.

We make you look good online, so you can sell stuff.

Our story

Our story began as two students who felt deeply inspired after attending a conference by Gary Vee.

He talked about the power of content and how leveraging on social media is essential for businesses and individuals to survive in future.

We realized that many business owners, coaches and people who wanted to create content didn’t have the skills to do so - but had tons of knowledge and stories they wanted to share from their experience.

We had a knack for breaking down complex ideas to simple ones... we thought this was something we had the skills and passion to help with.

We were inspired to take action.

One week later, we secured our first client.

And the rest is history.

Content creation is all about sharing knowledge, experience and information with the world.

Our mission is to help increase the career opportunities of 1,000,000 people within the next 5 years through our and our clients’ content.
